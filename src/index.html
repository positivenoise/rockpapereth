ROCK PAPER ETH is a simple 2 player head to head fight to the death via the ancient game of Rock Paper Scissors, the battle taking place on the most suitable of places, the grandoise world computer Ethereum. Battle it out with your friends and family for guts and glory, or simply out of boredem I don't really care bro what's your problem anyway.

To play, simply hit the register button above. Then sign the transaction that appears with Metamask. Each game state will ask you to sign another transaction. Refresh your browser after the transaction has completed. Enjoy!
